Our Story

About IKEA

Founded in 1943 in Sweden by Ingvar Kamprad, IKEA began as a mail-order business and grew into the world's largest furniture retailer, operating in 60+ countries with a mission to create a better everyday life for the many people.

IKEA India launched in 2018 with its first store in Hyderabad, bringing Scandinavian design and affordability to millions of Indian homes. Today, IKEA blends global quality with local sensibility.

  • Flat-pack design for easy transport & assembly
  • Sustainable materials and eco-friendly production
  • Affordable pricing for all income levels
  • Wide range from furniture to home accessories
